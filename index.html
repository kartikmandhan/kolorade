2. Once you complete level 1, another colour flashes. Hence to complete Level 2 , you must press the previous(level 1 colour) and the colour which flashed recently.
3. Similarly, Remember the sequence of colours as a new colour is added in each consecutive Levels.

5. Test your memory and see if you can score 800 points!
